WELCOME TO ADAM'S LCHAD PAGE!

Our son Adam is 26 years old and was diagnosed at 5 months of age with LCHAD a rare inherited fat metabolism disorder. In Adam's LCHAD Page we will try to tell you in simple terms just what LCHAD is and how Adam lives with it. You can read Adam’s Stories, which describe how Adam has managed the disease at certain ages. We will provide you with Links to other sites that might tell you more about LCHAD. Mostly we will give you the chance to meet our very special son who has a very rare disease. Welcome, and we hope to help you in whatever way we can!
